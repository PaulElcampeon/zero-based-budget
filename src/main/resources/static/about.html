ZERO-BASED BUDGET

WHAT IS IT?

Zero-Based Budgeting is a method where you allocate every dollar of your income to an expense or savings category. Unlike traditional budgeting, it starts from scratch each month, ensuring every expense is justified and prioritized.

BENEFITS

  • Optimal allocation of resources
  • Identifies unnecessary expenses
  • Increased financial discipline
  • Improved savings and investments

HOW CAN I DO IT?

  1. Create a list of income sources
  2. List all your expenses and savings categories
  3. Assign every dollar of income to these categories
  4. Track and monitor your spending regularly
  5. Adjust and optimize your budget as needed
